About Me

Hello! This is Md. Faraz Kabir Khan. I am currently pursuing a Master's in Information Technology at The University of Western Australia. With over 2.5 years of experience as a senior front-end web developer at BiTechX, I have had the privilege of serving clients across the globe, mostly from the USA and Canada. I feel thrilled working on innovative ideas and pioneering projects. During my undergraduate studies, I contributed significantly to two impactful projects: the Mars Rover, where I worked on AI features and managed the team's website, and the Driverless Formula Racing Car, where I collaborated with the team Circuit Maesters, and played a pivotal role in strategizing software and hardware frameworks, and data engineering process.

So far, I have worked with these technologies -

C/C++ Java Python JavaScript C# SQL HTML CSS/SCSS Bootstrap TailwindCSS Vue.js ASP.NET MVC WordPress Entity Kajabi Arduino PyTorch Tensorflow OpenCV & more...
